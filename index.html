Protect Our Oceans

The ocean covers more than 70% of our planet and produces over half of the oxygen we breathe. It is home to millions of species, many of which we haven’t even discovered yet.

Unfortunately, human activity has pushed marine ecosystems to the brink. Plastic waste, chemical spills, oil leaks, and untreated sewage are poisoning the waters that sustain life.

Every year, over 8 million tons of plastic enter the ocean — the equivalent of a garbage truck dumping trash into the sea every minute. If we don’t act now, there may be more plastic than fish by 2050.

Our Mission

Our goal is simple: to raise awareness about ocean pollution and inspire people to take action. We believe that education is the most powerful tool for change. When people understand the impact of pollution, they are more likely to make sustainable choices.

Through interactive tools like our pollution simulator, we help people visualize how harmful pollutants affect marine life. Fish lose their color, water becomes murky, and ecosystems collapse — all consequences of real-world pollution.

Whether you're a student, a teacher, or just someone who cares about the planet, we hope this project encourages you to protect our oceans and spread awareness in your community.
